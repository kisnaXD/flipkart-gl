What am I looking at?

This dashboard simulates how traffic police should respond to planned and sudden events. The map shows both real past incidents and a live scenario you can switch between.

1
Pick a scenario (left column) Each card is a realistic Bengaluru situation — cricket match, metro work, procession, VIP route, or flash congestion.
2
Read the map (center) Faded dots = past events from Astram data. The bright star = active event snapped to the nearest road. Circle = affected zone. B markers = barricades on real roads (intersections / known corridor points, not lakes). Blue lines = diversion routes along roads.
3
Use the action panel (right) Impact score tells you severity. Duration and delay estimate commuter pain. Constables/barricades are deployment recommendations. Hotspots show where similar incidents historically cluster.
